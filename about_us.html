About San Bernardino

At San Bernardino High, students have the opportunity to take Advanced Placement® course work and exams. The AP® participation rate at San Bernardino High is 53 percent. The student body makeup is 50 percent male and 50 percent female, and the total minority enrollment is 96 percent. San Bernardino High is 1 of 15 high schools in the San Bernardino City Unified.
